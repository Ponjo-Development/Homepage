ELIXIR PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated and effective: July 21, 2021.

This privacy policy applies to the Discord bot application Elixir#1902 (@838118537276031006), referred to as "Elixir", the "Ponjo Team", "us", or "we".

Your privacy is important to us. Our policy is to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our websites, services, and utilities. The Privacy Policies for each of our services, that of Elixir outlined below, provide a more detailed explanation of what data we store for each of our services, how long it is retained, how you can request for your data to be deleted, and more.

By default, Elixir stores little data on its own. Upon execution of the e!prefix command, the bot's new prefix, as specified by the command executor, is added & saved into a secure database. The only data stored upon execution of this command is the guild ID, automatically generated by Discord, of the server the command was executed in, and the contents of the prefix itself, which are encrypted.

With respect to users, Elixir only stores user IDs in its database only if the user is a subscriber to Elixir's premium services. If this is the case, the user's unique user ID (automatically generated by Discord) is added to the database securely.

It is important to note that this data is only stored as it is required for the bot to properly function. The data is not stored with anyone else and remains confidential, and is all safely secured in a database on an OVHCloud dedicated server.

ABOUT THE DATA WE STORE

Where is data stored/processed?

All data stored by Elixir is exclusively secured within our United States servers. While some of our other services use servers across the globe, data stored by Elixir is restricted to the United States. All data is secured and regularly backed up for privacy and to provide the best experience for users of the bot.

In addition, all data stored by Elixir is securely encrypted for privacy reasons. Your privacy is very important to us.

More Regarding Data Retention and Deletion

If you would like to submit a data access request in order to see all information Elixir has stored with respect to you, the user, you can do so by sending an email to Eerie, the team founder and developer, at eerie@ponjo.club or by contacting an administrator in our Discord support server, of which you can join by clicking here.

If you would like to request deletion of your data, you can do so by following the same procedure(s) as specified above.

It is important for you, the user, to know that we only retain data for as long as deemed necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Upon identity verification that it is indeed you, the user, who is requesting to have your data deleted, we will proceed with the process and all data we store with respect to you, the user, will be deleted promptly.

2. With your consent, we may disclose your information.
