LGBTfy - Pride Avatar Generator

Welcome to LGBTfy, a simple LGBTQIA+ pride avatar generator. Easily express yourself by adding pride flag colors & flairs around your profile picture.

The process of adding pride flairs to your profile picture is extremely simple. First, choose the appropriate flairs you would like to add to the borders of your avatar. Next, upload your avatar to the website. Then, you can modify the size of the avatar and the orientation of the pride flairs and stripes. Finally, save your new avatar to your computer by clicking the button "Download new avatar as JPEG!". Sweet, you now have a brand new, flairy pride avatar!

The Meaning of Pride

"There will not be a magic day when we wake up and it’s now okay to express ourselves publicly. We make that day by doing things publicly until it’s simply the way things are." ─ Tammy Baldwin

"Pride means having the courage to be authentic and live your truth even if you’re scared for your life or scared of being an outsider. Pride is being aware that it’s OK to be who you are, even if you don’t see validation of that. It's knowing that, wherever you go, there will always be those who will reject you for who you are, but that there will also be those who accept the LGBT community and support you. It's using that struggle as a driving force to express our own identities, no matter what. It's the ability and freedom to be who we are and openly love those we choose without shame. It's remembering that life always gets better, and no matter what, there are always people who will support you and care for you. Pride is the freedom to be ourselves & accepting ourselves for who we are." ─ Ben Petrillo
